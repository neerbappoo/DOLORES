RESTORE TRAVEL CONFIDENCE – MALDIVES

A series of short interviews with hospitality and industry leaders on the topic of how we can effectively communicate with our audience to help them restore travel confidence across the Indian Ocean

In this interview:
Ms. Yusra Ibrahim
Marketing Manager – Orca Media GRoup in charge of Travel Trade Maldives and TTM Trade Show 24-26 November 2020
In conversation with Dolores, Yusra gave his insights on three key questions:
– what does then new normal look and feel?
– what is the key message that should be communicated to restore travel confidence?
– what actions were taken by her company to help customers build their confidence again?
