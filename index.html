Podcast

A weekly interview series in conversation with tourism industry leaders and travel professionals about what goes on in the industry today, how do we embark on the journey to travel recovery and restore confidence in travel.

Whether you are a hospitality professional or a passionate traveller looking for a podcast that gives you more than just wanderlust, my tourism industry guests from all around the world, walk you through the journey to travel recovery of their countries and share their priceless insights on the destination they live and work in.
