ABOUT DOLORES

Dolores Semeraro is a hospitality keynote speaker on the topic of ‘Learn how to speak the digital language of your audience’ and a former Marketing and PR professional. In her decade- long career in the tourism and travel industry, it was Dolores’ job to create and execute cross-cultural communication strategies. She now speaks to organizations and travel institutions around the world on the connection techniques that successfully turn passive audiences into brand advocates.

Dolores also has a Translating and Interpreting degree in Chinese and English Language. She has mastered Mandarin at the Beijing Language and CultureUniversity in China.

Having lived in Beijing, Shenzhen, The Maldives and The Republic of Mauritius, Dolores has deep intercultural understandings, which allows her to communicate and appeal to a variety of audiences. She has worked for some of the most established hotel companies across Asia and has been featured in several publications both in Maldives and Mauritius. She has an engaged social media community and masters the art of knotting and tea drinking.
